M.Tech./M.Sc. Admissions

SCEE, IIT Mandi MTech admissions process
  1. Candidates have filled up the M.Tech admission form with details such as COAP ID, GATE details, specialization etc.
  2. Based on the shortlisting / ranking criteria, in each admission round, the specialization and category wise shortlisted candidates will be informed to COAP.
  3. The candidates have to access the COAP website for considering and finalizing their admission options on specified dates by COAP.
  4. The above process continues until the round in which the seats at IIT Mandi are filled.
